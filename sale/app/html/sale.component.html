Autonomous, Decentralized & Automated

FirstBlood will be the first decentralized app, built on top of Ethereum, that allows eSports enthusiasts to compete in their favorite games through a decentralized, automated platform.


FirstBlood aims to be the preferred global platform for skill-based eSports competitors and to play an essential part in shaping the future of blockchain-based projects, and it needs your support!

What's FirstBlood Token?

FirstBlood Token (“1SŦ”) is an essential part of the FirstBlood ecosystem and economy. The four major utilities available to FirstBlood Token holders are:


(1) Playing matches;

(2) Witnessing matches and voting on the jury;

(3) Hosting tournaments;

(4) Claiming rewards from referrals;

Funding Breakdown

The proceeds from this software sale will be primarily used for funding the development of FirstBlood beyond its initial release to cover costs from the development of the technologies, paying for servers, paying for bandwidth, marketing, organizing eSports events as well as business development and legal fees.
